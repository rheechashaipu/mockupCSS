Teemo

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Scientific Name: Belial Father of Lies
  • Average Length: 100 units
  • Fun fact!: Riot Games uses Teemo as a unit of measurement when creating game objects for League of Legends
  • Average Lifespan: Eternal
  • Habitat: Top lane of Summoner's Rift

Teemo's main advantage is being able to play against melee ranged characters by utilizing hit and run tactics.
